About Us

Lexicon House is a premier English language academy dedicated to delivering refined and effective instruction. We believe that language is not just about words—it is culture, thought, and a way of life. Our programs are crafted to serve learners of all ages through immersive and professional experiences.

Our Programs

  • Lexicon House Kids: Ages 5–11 – Interactive learning with games, stories, and movement-based activities.
  • Lexicon House Teens: Ages 12–17 – Academic development and communication skills.
  • Lexicon House Pro: Young Adults & Professionals – Business English, writing, and presentation skills.
  • Lexicon IELTS: IELTS exam preparation using modern strategies.
  • Lexicon Club: Conversation sessions and workshops for all ages.

  • Interactive materials and professional tools
